# Brand Kit: Personalised Training Plans

## 1) Brand Foundation Sheet

### Primary Identity
- Primary brand name: `Personalised Training Plans`
- Short name: `Personalised Plans`
- Domain-facing identity: `coachedclimbing.com`

### Prohibited Variants
- Do not use as visible UI brand name:
  - `AI Climbing Coach`
  - `AI Coach`
  - `Intelligent Coach`

### One-Sentence Positioning
`Personalised Training Plans helps climbers build realistic, structured training blocks they can adjust week to week with coach-guided support.`

### Tone Pillars
- Practical: clear instructions over hype.
- Supportive: coaching language that builds confidence.
- Grounded: tie advice to schedule, recovery, and real constraints.
- Action-focused: emphasise what to do next.

### Say / Avoid Saying
- Say:
  - `Build your next block`
  - `Adjust sessions`
  - `Track progress`
  - `Built around your week`
- Avoid:
  - `Revolutionary AI`
  - `Magic`
  - `Instant transformation`
  - `Fully automated coaching`

### UK Spelling Rules
- Always use:
  - `personalised`
  - `optimised`
  - `prioritise`
- Prefer:
  - `Log in` (verb phrase) instead of `Login` in action labels
- Keep technical terms unchanged where required by APIs/code.

### Approved Terminology
- `Plan`, `Block`, `Session`, `Summary`, `Coach chat`, `Progress`, `Feedback`

## 3) Homepage Messaging Pack

### Hero
- Kicker: `Personalised Training Plans`
- Headline: `Personalised climbing blocks, built around your week.`
- Subheadline: `Answer a short onboarding questionnaire to build a structured plan, adjust sessions with coach chat, and track what you complete.`
- Primary CTA: `Get started`
- Secondary CTA: `Log in`
- Tertiary CTA: `How it works`

### How It Works
1. `Create your plan`  
   `Name your next block and keep each regenerated version for reference.`
2. `Answer onboarding`  
   `Share your discipline, schedule, goals, and constraints so your plan fits real life.`
3. `Generate your block`  
   `Get a clear week-by-week structure with sessions you can actually execute.`
4. `Adjust and complete`  
   `Use coach chat to refine sessions, then track what you complete each week.`

### What You Get
- `Structured plans` — `Consistent session structure that is easy to scan and follow.`
- `Plan-specific onboarding` — `Each plan keeps its own onboarding context and version history.`
- `Coach chat adjustments` — `Request substitutions and intensity changes without rewriting the full block.`
- `Progress tracking` — `Track completed sessions and keep notes to stay consistent.`
- `Safe regeneration` — `Generate a new plan version without losing earlier work.`
- `Built for real schedules` — `Adapt when travel, fatigue, or time constraints affect training.`
